A list of Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) has been provided below for new or confused players.

Q: What is 16.05?

A: 16.05 is an official total conversion modification of Minecraft Alpha 1.0_16.05. It is where ideas that don't get implemented into the main branch end up.

Q: What was the point of "recruitment"?

A: The way [0] went around deploying this was a bit of a social experiment. We wanted to see what people would do and if they would even notice that they're divided across two different versions.

Q: What is the point of 16.05?

A: 16.05 relies heavily on online play. This is not something the creators of the original branch want, so the code to experiment with this was branched off to our team.

Q: What platforms is Minecraft available on?

A: Currently, we support Windows Vista and above. There are no official downloads for Apple and Linux systems yet.
